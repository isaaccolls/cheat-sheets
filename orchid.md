# care

- indoor plant
- keep in original pot to favor light entry to the roots
- do not change the substrate (do not add any type of soil)
- do not expose to direct sunlight
- average temperature 20°C (range from 16°C to 30°C)

# when to water?

- when the root is white-grayish in color
- when the substrate is completely dry
- let the water drain completely
- on hot days it is suggested to additionally spray the leaves with water besides watering
- do not wet the flowers
